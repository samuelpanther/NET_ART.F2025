A 10+ hour marathon YouTube video of Molly reading aloud every single unread message from her already famous Tumblr. With a straight face, no differentiation between messages/senders, the messages take on an absurd life of their own as they envelop Molly, exposing her role as mirror and object of projection with all of the expectations, kindnesses, pick-up lines, cruelties, and a myriad of other awkwardnesses leading emotional and physical exhaustion in both viewer and artist.
