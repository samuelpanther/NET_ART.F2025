Friday Night

It's been a long, hard week.

You are cooked.

You are also in desperate need of fun.

The choice isn't an easy one.

Do you spend some much needed decompression time at home?

Or do you take advantage of your freedom for the weekend, message the gang, and see who's up for a night on the town?

Think about this, because Friday nights are precious.
